Frequently Asked Questions About Financing

What do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid government-issued ID, proof of income, and a few basic personal details. The application can be completed in our showroom or over the phone.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Initial pre-qualification typically involves a soft credit check, which does not impact your credit score. A hard inquiry may be required upon final approval for certain financing options.

Can I pay off my balance early?

Yes! Most of our financing plans allow early payoff without penalties. Paying early can save you money on interest charges.

What if I'm not approved?

We work with multiple financing partners, so if one option doesn't work, we'll explore alternatives. Our team is committed to finding a solution that works for you.
