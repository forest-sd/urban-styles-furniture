Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with Urban Styles Furniture.

Shopping & Showroom

Where is Urban Styles Furniture located?

Our showroom is at 8375 Castleton Corner Dr, Indianapolis, IN 46250 — conveniently located in the Castleton area. Walk-ins are always welcome; no appointment necessary.

Do I need an appointment to visit?

No appointment needed! Our showroom is open during regular business hours and our team is always ready to help. However, if you'd like dedicated one-on-one design guidance, calling ahead at (317) 578-7770 ensures we can give you our full attention.

Do you offer free design guidance?

Absolutely. Our team provides complimentary, personalized design guidance to help you choose furniture that fits your space, style, and budget. Whether you're furnishing one room or an entire home, we're here to help.

Can I see fabric and material samples in person?

Yes! Our showroom features a wide selection of furniture on display so you can see, touch, and test pieces before buying. We also have fabric swatches and material samples for many of our collections.

Delivery & Pickup

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by product and availability. Many in-stock items can be delivered within a few business days. Custom orders and special-order pieces may take longer. Our team will provide a specific estimated delivery date at the time of purchase.

Do you deliver outside of Indianapolis?

We primarily deliver to the greater Indianapolis area. For deliveries outside our standard zone, please contact us to discuss options and any additional fees that may apply.

Can I pick up my furniture instead of having it delivered?

Yes! In-store pickup is available for most items. When your order is ready, we'll contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time. Please bring a vehicle appropriate for the size of your purchase.

What if my furniture arrives damaged?

We take great care in packaging and delivering your furniture. If any item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours at (317) 578-7770 or info@shopurbanstyles.com. We'll arrange a replacement or repair at no additional cost.

Financing & Payment

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options with fast approval and competitive rates. Multiple plans are available to fit different budgets and credit situations. Visit our financing page for more details or ask in-store.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and approved financing. Personal checks may be accepted with valid identification.

Is a deposit required?

Deposit requirements vary by order type. Many of our financing options require no down payment. For special orders, a deposit may be required to begin production. Your sales associate will explain all terms clearly.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We want you to love your furniture. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 48 hours of delivery to discuss options. Return eligibility and conditions may vary depending on the product type and any customization involved.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Many of our furniture pieces include manufacturer warranties. Warranty terms vary by brand and product. Ask your sales associate for specific warranty information on any item you're considering.

Can I exchange an item?

Exchanges may be available depending on the product and circumstances. Contact us as soon as possible if you'd like to discuss an exchange. Our goal is to ensure you're happy with your purchase.

Products & Services

What types of furniture do you sell?

We carry a full range of modern home furniture including living room sets, bedroom collections, dining furniture, home office pieces, mattresses, and home décor. Browse our products page for a complete overview.

Do you carry mattresses?

Yes! We offer a selection of premium mattresses including memory foam, innerspring, and hybrid options in all standard sizes. Visit our showroom to test different models and find your perfect sleep surface.

Can you help me furnish an entire home?

That's one of our specialties. Our team can help you coordinate furniture and décor across every room in your home, ensuring a cohesive style that reflects your personality. We offer guidance on layout, color coordination, and piece selection.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help. Reach out anytime.
