Designing Your Living Room

A well-designed living room starts with a plan. Here are our top tips:

  • Anchor with the sofa: Position your sofa first ‚Äî it's the largest piece and defines the room's layout. Face it toward your focal point (TV, fireplace, or window).
  • Create conversation zones: Arrange seating so people face each other. An accent chair angled toward the sofa creates a natural conversation area.
  • Scale matters: A massive sectional in a small room feels cramped. A petite loveseat in a large room feels lost. Match furniture scale to room size.
  • Layer lighting: Combine overhead lighting with floor lamps and table lamps. This creates warmth and lets you adjust the mood for different activities.
  • Add a rug: A rug anchors the seating area and adds texture. It should be large enough that at least the front legs of your sofa and chairs rest on it.

Not sure where to start? Our team provides free design guidance. Visit our showroom and we'll help you plan the perfect living room layout.
