Bedroom Design Tips

  • Start with the bed: It's the focal point. Choose your bed frame and headboard first, then build the rest of the room around it.
  • Symmetry creates calm: Matching nightstands on each side of the bed create a balanced, restful feel. They don't need to be identical â€” just similar in scale.
  • Invest in quality bedding: The best bedroom furniture in the world won't help if your sheets and pillows are uncomfortable. Layer your bedding for both comfort and visual appeal.
  • Keep it clutter-free: Choose furniture with built-in storage. Platform beds with drawers, nightstands with shelves, and dressers with organizers help maintain a serene environment.
  • Soft lighting: Overhead lighting in bedrooms should be dimmable. Add bedside lamps for reading and ambient light that sets a relaxing mood.
